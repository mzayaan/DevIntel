Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Overview

DevIntel is a developer news aggregator that collects and displays publicly available content from Dev.to, GitHub Trending, and Hacker News. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require account registration.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not track your reading activity.
  • We do not use advertising networks or sell data to third parties.
  • We do not use analytics services (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.).

Local Storage

DevIntel stores the following data only on your device using your browser's localStorage:

  • Theme preference — whether you selected dark or light mode.
  • Bookmarked articles — article IDs you have saved for later reading.
  • API response cache — news content cached for up to 10 minutes to reduce network requests and support offline use.

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Server-Side Caching

Our server proxies requests to Dev.to, GitHub, and Hacker News and caches the responses in memory for a short period (typically 5–15 minutes). This cache contains only publicly available news content — no user-specific data.

Third-Party Content

DevIntel displays content sourced from:

  • Dev.to — developer articles and posts
  • GitHub — trending repositories
  • Hacker News — community news links

Clicking external links will take you to these third-party sites, which have their own privacy policies.

Service Worker & Offline Support

DevIntel registers a service worker to enable offline functionality. The service worker caches static app assets (HTML, CSS, JS, icons) and recent API responses locally on your device. No data is sent externally by the service worker.

Children's Privacy

DevIntel does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no user-generated content, no registration, and no communication features.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "last updated" date at the top of this page will change. Since we collect no personal data, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy.
